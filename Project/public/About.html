The Merchant's Guild

The Guild was formed in honor of a meek and humble man named Greirat. In Greirat's absence, the Guild continues to return valuable and rare items, left behind by lost adventurers, to those with the courage to venture into the dark and twisted world.

Greirat

Hailing from the Undead Settlement, Greirat was a thief and a merchant who was once imprisoned in the Tower of the High Wall of Lothric. He fancied himself a Martyr of the poor, and nearly decended into madness after the discovery of the death of his beloved Loretta. He set himself on a quest of uncovering the secrets of the world and providing them to the weary travelers who sought them.
